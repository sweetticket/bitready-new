Credit card fees are high

Retailers spend millions in credit card processing fees every year. However, Bitcoin payments are processed for free. Many stores choose to pass the savings on to the customer in the form of a discount, usually 3-5%.

We believe you should have access to these discounts without the headache of signing up with a Bitcoin exchange and managing every transfer. At BitReady, we charge you the dollar amount of your purchase and send bitcoins on your behalf. By doing this, we allow you to experience all the benefits of Bitcoin without needing to directly interface with it.
